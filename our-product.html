Cold Processed Extra Virgin Coconut Oil: A Purity-Preserving Journey

Process

Our cold-processed Extra Virgin Coconut Oil goes through a meticulous, low-temperature journey that retains the oil's natural purity and health benefits. Here's how it's done

01
Handpicked Coconuts

Our process begins with the careful selection of mature, organic coconuts from our lush groves. Only the finest coconuts make the cut.

02
Fresh Meat Extraction

The coconut meat is expertly extracted from the coconuts. This step ensures that only the freshest, high-quality coconut meat is used for the oil.

03
Cold Extraction

In cold extraction, coconut oil is obtained from fresh meat without heat, preserving natural qualities at temperatures below 120°F (49°C)

04
Natural Settling

After extraction, oil naturally settles, separating impurities from the pure oil, ensuring a pristine final product.

05
Filtration

The oil is carefully filtered to remove any remaining impurities while retaining its natural aroma and taste.

06
Packaging

We package Extra Virgin Coconut Oil in eco-friendly containers to maintain freshness and purity, reflecting our commitment to sustainability.

07
Quality Assurance

Prior to delivery, our cold-processed Extra Virgin Coconut Oil undergoes rigorous quality checks, ensuring only the finest reaches your table.
